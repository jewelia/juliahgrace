Speaking

I regularly give talks at conferences, companies, and meetups. I've spoken in front of thousands at Web 2.0 to much smaller groups at meetups, and have experience moderating panels as well.

If you’d like me to speak at your conference, company, or meetup, please note:

  • Companies – I charge a fee for speaking at companies.
  • Conferences – Negotiable depending on the conference, location, amount of travel required, etc. I generally will find a way to make it to conferences if my schedule allows.
  • Meetups – No charge if I’m already in the area and able to make it.
  • Other – Contact me and let’s discuss.
